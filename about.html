About Sole DXB

Sole DXB is an independent community-driven platform that is the leading voice for youth and contemporary culture across the Middle East and North Africa. Established in 2011, Sole DXB has become a cultural phenomenon, attracting sneaker enthusiasts, streetwear aficionados, artists, musicians, and creatives from around the world.

The event showcases a diverse range of activities, including sneaker exhibitions, street art installations, live music performances, panel discussions, and workshops. It serves as a hub for collaboration and networking among like-minded individuals who share a passion to be themselves.

Sole DXB has played a significant role in shaping the cultural landscape of the region, providing a platform for emerging talents and fostering a sense of community among its attendees. The event continues to evolve, embracing new trends and pushing the boundaries of creativity.
